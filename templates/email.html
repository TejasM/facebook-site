OFFICIAL CONTEST RULES

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. A PURCHASE WILL NOT INCREASE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING. The BeneSelfies contest (the "Contest") is sponsored by Benefit Cosmetics Services Canada Inc. (the “Sponsor”). For the purposes of the Contest, the “Contest Group” is composed of the Sponsor, Samsung Electronics Canada Inc., West 49 Inc. together with their respective affiliates and related companies, including without limitation their parent, sister and subsidiary companies, advertising and promotion agencies, suppliers of material and services related to the Contest, and any other corporation, partnership, sole proprietorship or other legal entity directly involved in the Contest and their respective officers, directors, employees, agents, and other representatives.

1. CONTEST PERIOD

The Contest starts on October 7, 2013 at 9:00:00 AM Eastern Time ("ET") and ends on November 3, 2013 at 11:59:59 PM ET (the " Contest Period").

2. ELIGIBILITY

The Contest is open to legal residents of Canada who have reached the age of majority in the province or territory in which they reside as of the date of entry. Excluded from eligibility are officers, directors, employees, agents and representatives of Sponsor, any independent contest judging organization, each of their respective parent, affiliated and related companies, agencies, suppliers of the materials and services related to this Contest, and members of any immediate families (defined as parents, siblings, children and spouses, regardless of where they live) or households (whether or not related) of such officers, directors, employees, agents and representatives.

3. HOW TO ENTER

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY. Internet access and valid email account required. Online entry through the Contest WebPage (as defined below) only; no other means of submission will be accepted. You can enter the Contest by visiting the Benefit Cosmetic Canada’s BeneSelfies webpage at www.beneselfies.com (the “Contest WebPage”). To enter the Contest, go to the Contest WebPage and follow these steps:

Step One : On the landing page, click “play now!”

Step Two : Under “How to Play” click “okay!”

Step Three : Enter your first name, last name and email address in the fields provided.

Step Four : Confirm that you have read and agree to these Official Contest Rules.

Step Five : Click on the “Like” button to Like the Benefit Cosmetics Canada’s Facebook page (the “Registration”).

Limit : One (1) Registration per person during the Contest Period. For clarity, you will receive one (1) Entry (as defined below) into the Contest upon Registration, but Registration need only be completed once when you initially register for the Contest; thereafter, to enter the Contest you will not be required to perform Step Four above (each an “Entry” and collectively, the “Entries”). For the purposes of this Contest and these Official Contest Rules an “Entry” and collectively, the “Entries”, shall include the “ Registration”, as each term is defined herein.

Limit : one (1) Entry per person per day during the Contest Period. You can only use one (1) email address and one (1) Facebook account in connection with the Contest. Each Entry must be submitted by the entrant him or herself. Entries must comply with Facebook.com terms and conditions to be eligible. Any Entries that are made, or suspected to have been made, through automated means or scripts will be disqualified in Sponsor’s sole discretion. The clock used by the Sponsor in connection with the receipt of the Entries will be the official clock to determine the date and time the Entry was received. Entries must be received during the Contest Period. For the purposes of submitting an Entry in this Contest in accordance with these Official Contest Rules, a “day” shall mean any 24-hour period from the last time entrant submits an Entry during the Contest Period.

By entering this Contest, you agree to be bound by these Official Contest Rules and the decisions of the Sponsor and/or Contest judges.

By participating in this Contest, each entrant represents and warrants that the Photos tagged by the entrant shall comply with the following.

  1. Does not include the brand names, trade names, or trade-marks of any third parties;

  2. Does not include any reference to or likeness of any celebrity or any identifiable third parties other than the entrant and his/her Friends;

  3. Is suitable for family audiences and for display and publication on the Contest WebPage, in the sole determination of the Sponsor. Without limitation, Photos shall not contain any content that is or contains: the promotion of unlawful behaviour, profanity, nudity, explicit sexuality, harmful, threatening, abusive, harassing, tortious, defamatory, vulgar, obscene, libelous, or is hatefully, racially, ethnically or otherwise offensive or objectionable;

  4. Does not depict illegal activities or promotion thereof;

  5. Is consistent with the Sponsor’s corporate image; and

  6. Complies with these Official Contest Rules.

The Sponsor reserves the right to deem ineligible and exclude from the Contest any Photo that the Sponsor, in its sole and absolute discretion, determines is not befitting or compliant with the Submission Guidelines and/or these Official Contest Rules.

By entering this Contest and tagging a Photo, you acknowledge and agree that the tagged Photo will be used on the Contest WebPage for public viewing, including, without limitation, by showing up in your and your Friends news feed. You hereby irrevocably, perpetually, and without limitation, grant the Sponsor the unlimited right and permission to use, post, publish, adapt, edit and/or modify such Photo (and name in connection with your Photo) and waive all your rights, including moral rights, in and to the Photo, without notice or compensation.
