NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO TAKE PART IN THE I AM TOM* PROJECT. A PURCHASE WILL NOT INCREASE YOUR CHANCES OF YOUR ENTRY BEING FEATURED DURING TOM*. 1. Project Period

The Project begins on July 18th at 9 AM Eastern Standard Time (“EST”) and ends on August 15th at 5 PM EST (the “ Project Period”).

2. Participation

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY. The Project is open to everyone. Internet access and a valid social media account (Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram) required. You can take part in the Project by visiting the I AM TOM* Project webpage at http://iamtom.ca/ (the “ Project Webpage”). To participate in the Project, go to the Project Webpage and follow these steps:

Step 1: On the landing page, select the social media platform you would like to use.

Step 2: Sign in to your account if necessary.

Step 3: Authorize Toronto Men’s Fashion to use your account.

Step 4: Upload or take a photo.

Step 5: Customize your photo.

Step 6: Share your photo using any of the available social media platforms.

You can submit as many photos (the “Submission”) as you like. Photo submission must comply with Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter terms and conditions to be eligible to be a part of the Project. Any Submissions that are made which violate Facebook, Instagram, and/or Twitter terms and conditions or community standards will be discarded in Sponsor’s sole discretion. All Submissions must adhere to the Submission Guidelines, as defined below.

By participating in this Project, each entrant represents and warrants that the Photos tagged by the entrant (the “Photos”) shall comply with the following.

  1. Does not include the brand names, trade names, or trade-marks of any third parties;

  3. Is suitable for family audiences and for display and publication on the Project Webpage, in the sole determination of the Sponsor. Without limitation, photos shall not contain any content that is or contains: the promotion of unlawful behaviour, profanity, nudity, explicit sexuality, harmful, threatening, abusive, harassing, tortious, defamatory, vulgar, obscene, libelous, or is hatefully, racially, ethnically or otherwise offensive or objectionable;

  4. Does not depict illegal activities or promotion thereof;

  6. Complies with these Official Project Rules.

The Sponsor reserves the right to deem ineligible and exclude from the Project any Photo that the Sponsor, in its sole and absolute discretion, determines is not befitting or compliant with the Submission Guidelines and/or these Official Project Rules.
